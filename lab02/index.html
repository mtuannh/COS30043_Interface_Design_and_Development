WAI FILMS LAB COMPANY

Located in the creative heart of Melbourne, WAI Films is more than just a processing lab; it is a dedicated sanctuary for the analog soul. We understand that every frame of film captures a unique moment in time, which is why we treat every roll with the utmost precision and artistic care. From high-quality C-41 developing and professional-grade scanning to providing a curated selection of fresh, expired, and cine film stocks, our mission is to keep the craft of film photography thriving. Whether you are a seasoned professional or a curious beginner exploring the streets of Melbourne, we invite you to trust us with your vision. At WAI Films, we don't just develop film—we bring your stories to life with the timeless texture and depth that only analog can provide.

Why choose us?

Expert Processing

Our lab technicians use industry-standard C-41 and B&W chemistry to ensure your negatives are perfect every time.

High-Res Scanning

We use Noritsu and Frontier scanners to deliver sharp, color-accurate digital files that bring your film to life.

Preserving History

We specialize in handling expired stocks and rare formats with the care they deserve to capture that vintage look.

Fast Turnaround

Based in the heart of Melbourne, we offer quick local drop-offs and mail-in services with a 48-hour digital delivery.

Analog Passion

We aren't just a business; we are photographers. We test every film stock we sell to provide you with expert advice.

Eco-Friendly

We use a silver recovery system and responsibly dispose of all chemicals to keep Melbourne’s environment clean.
